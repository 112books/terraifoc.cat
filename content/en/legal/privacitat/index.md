## Data we collect

This website **does not collect personal data** directly, except:

### Contact form

If you contact us through the form, we will receive your name, email and message. This data is used exclusively to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

The data is processed by [Formspree](https://formspree.io) as a data processor, based in the United States. Formspree complies with the EU Privacy Framework for international data transfers.

**Legal basis:** express consent when submitting the form (Art. 6.1.a GDPR).  
**Retention:** data is retained for as long as necessary to respond to the inquiry, and at most 12 months.

### Web analytics

We use [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com), an analytics service that **does not use cookies**, **does not collect personal data**, and **anonymizes IP addresses**. GoatCounter only records visits, page views and aggregate technical data (browser, operating system, device). No individual user identification is possible.

**Legal basis:** legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f GDPR) — measuring and improving site usage without compromising privacy.
