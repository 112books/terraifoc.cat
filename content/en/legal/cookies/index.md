## What are cookies?

Cookies are files that are downloaded and stored on the user's device (computer, tablet, mobile phone) when visiting a website, in order to remember the visit and store information about the browsing session.

## Cookies on this website

This website **does not use cookies** of any kind:

- **No first-party cookies:** We do not store any cookies in your browser.
- **No third-party cookies:** We do not use services that install cookies (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.).
- **No advertising cookies:** We do not display advertising or do remarketing.

## Cookie-free analytics

We use [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com), a web analytics service **that does not use cookies**. GoatCounter works without persistent identifiers and anonymizes IP addresses, so that no individual user can be identified.

You can review [GoatCounter's privacy policy](https://www.goatcounter.com/privacy) for more information.

## Consequences of not using cookies

Since we do not use cookies, **there is no** need to accept or reject any cookie banner. Your browsing on this site does not generate any persistent trace in your browser.

This policy is prepared in accordance with:

- **Organic Law 3/2018** on Personal Data Protection and guarantee of digital rights (LOPDGDD, Spain).
- **Directive 2002/58/EC** (as amended by Directive 2009/136/EC) on the use of cookies.
